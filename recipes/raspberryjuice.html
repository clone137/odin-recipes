Raspberry Juice

Description

Drink from The Witcher 3 - Wild Hunt

Ingredients

  • 62.5g raspberries
  • 118ml water
  • 62.5 ml honey (fresh, high-grade)

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring raspberries and water to a boil
  2. Reduce heat and let simmer (about 3 minutes). Stir in honey
  3. Remove from heat, and let cool
  4. Strain, reserving the juice
